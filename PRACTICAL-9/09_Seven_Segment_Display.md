AIM : 

 By Using the Seven Segment Display to glow the Number 2.

COURSE OF ACTION :

We want to check the SSD is Anode or Cathode, if it is Anode give the 5v to Middle pin of the SSD else connect the GND pin to Middle pin of the SSD.
In this Experiment we are going to Display Number 2 in SSD.
If we have anode SSD give HIGH to pin number 4 and 7 ,LOW to all other pins.
If we have cathode SSD give LOW to pin number 4 and 7 ,HIGH to all other pins.
