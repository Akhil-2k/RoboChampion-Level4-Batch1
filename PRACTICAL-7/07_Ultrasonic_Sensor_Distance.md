AIM :

  To check the object is presence in-front of the ultrasonic.  

COURSE OF ACTION :

First we Vcc to 5v and Gnd to Gnd, Trig to 11 and Echo to 12. 
Trig pin is used to trigger the Ultrasonic sound pulses.
Echo pin is receive the signal of pulses. The Length of the pulse is proportional to the time it took for the transmitted signal to be detected.          	
It show the Object is present in-front of the Ultrasonic.
